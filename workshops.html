The Outcome

A live site, plus the know-how to build the next one solo.

By the end of the workshop, every participant will have built their first web page with AI-assisted coding in VSCode, pushed it to GitHub, and deployed it live to Vercel. They'll know the full workflow end to end, and leave with a written step-by-step guide.

The Audience

For the people who've always wanted to just try it.

Product managers, designers, marketers, ops, founders' office: anyone who's had an idea for a small tool or landing page and had to wait on engineering. Vibe coding doesn't turn your team into engineers, it gives them the confidence to build small things themselves.

The Customization

Shaped around what your team actually wants to build.

Every workshop is tailored before we start. We'll talk through what your team wants to ship: internal tools, landing pages, customer-facing prototypes, quick experiments. Your team leaves with skills they'll use on Monday, not generic ones they'll forget by Friday.

The Logistics

How It Runs

  • Two hours on-site
  • Up to ten people per cohort
  • Mac only
  • Every participant gets a printed step-by-step guide to take home
