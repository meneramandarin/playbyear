Why It Works

The Person on the Mic Shapes the Whole Event

Energy without cringe

Enough presence to lift the room, without turning the event into forced corporate theatre.

Technical fluency

AI, crypto, developer tools, startups, and creative technology are not treated as alien subjects. The host understands the context and can translate it for the room.

Calm in live chaos

Schedule changes, AV issues, late speakers, nervous founders, overlong panels — live events always wobble. The job is to keep the audience feeling held anyway.
